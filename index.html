VICTORIOUS KIDSS EDUCARES

Victorious Kidss Educares is an IB World School located in Kharadi, Pune, India. It was established by Dr. Robbin Ghosh in January 1997. The school is driven by the motto of ‘Learning to Love to Learn’, and strives to create ‘lifelong learners’. It is a co-educational school from Playgroup up to Grade 12th.
